Kevin J. Miller is a photographer who works out of north central Connecticut. His predominant fields of expertise include but are by no means limited to: automotive photography (indoor or outdoor), portrait photography, cityscapes, landscapes, and lifestyles. He has been shooting photos professionally for about four years and is extremely dedicated to his work.

If you would like to schedule a photo shoot please visit the Contact section of the site and send Kevin a message!
